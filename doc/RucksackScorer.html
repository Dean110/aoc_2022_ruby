Overview

RucksackScorer is for the third day of Advent of Code 2022. The problem was to read a list of strings representing elf rucksacks, finding common items, scoring them and submitting the sum of the items.

This method parses the rucksack inputs and divides the list into teams of elves. The teams of elves then have their rucksacks examined to find their badge items. The badge items are assigned a priority score and then all of the teams’ badges’ scores are summed.

def score_all_badges
  elf_teams = @rucksacks.each_slice(3).to_a
  elf_teams.each.map do |team|
    badge = find_badge team
    translate_item_priority badge
  end.sum
end

#score_all_rucksacks ⇒ Integer

This method parses the rucksack inputs and finds a common character between the first and second half of the String. It then assigns a priority score to the common item for each rucksack and sums all of the common items’ scores.
